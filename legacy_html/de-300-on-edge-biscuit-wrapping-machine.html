Product Description

Overview

The DE-300 is one of the most reliable and successful biscuit wrapping machines by D.E. Technics. Designed for bakery items and biscuits standing on edge, it supports a wide range of shapes including round, square, rectangular, and oval. Its L-Type conveyor feeding ensures consistent spacing and positioning of products, making it suitable for automated lines. With a capacity of up to 80 packs per minute and durable construction, this machine is engineered for high productivity and simple adjustability during production.

Features

  • Supports a variety of biscuit shapes
  • High-efficiency L-Type feeding system
  • Simplified product changeover
  • Compact structure with strong build
  • Up to 80 packs per minute performance

Applications

  • Bakery item packaging (standing orientation)
