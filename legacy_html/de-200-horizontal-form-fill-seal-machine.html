Product Description

Overview

The DE-200 is a versatile, continuous-motion horizontal form-fill-seal (HFFS) machine. Designed for medium-sized products, it efficiently wraps items like biscuits, chocolate bars, toffee bars, ice lollies, soap, and more in pillow-type packs. The machine ensures print registration accuracy through its integrated photo-electric eye and inverter control. With user-friendly design, the DE-200 offers ease of operation, fast changeovers, and high reliability in production lines.

Features

  • High-speed horizontal packing up to 200 ppm
  • Consistent sealing performance
  • Accurate print registration
  • Compact footprint with robust frame
  • Easy maintenance and operator-friendly interface

Technical Specifications

Size of Pack (L x W x H) 80–200 x 40–80 x 10–30 mm
Output Up to 200 packs per minute
Electric Motors (1) 1 H.P.
(2) ½ H.P.
Heaters 1.6 Kw
Standard Equipment Photo Electric Eye & Inverter
Dimensions of Machine 3,990 x 900 x 1,650 mm
Weight 800 kg
Reel (Max Outer Dia / Core Dia) 300 / 75 mm

Applications

  • Pillow wrapping of biscuits, soap, candy bars, ice lollies, etc.
  • Suitable for FMCG industries with mid-sized product lines
