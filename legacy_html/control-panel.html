Product Description

Overview

The CONTROL PANEL serves as the nerve center of machinery and equipment operations, offering a centralized interface for monitoring and managing diverse functions. Equipped with advanced technology and intuitive controls, it provides operators with precise control over parameters such as temperature, speed, and output. The CONTROL PANEL streamlines operations, enhances productivity, and ensures optimal performance across industrial settings.

Control Panel (Optional Accessory)

This Control Panel is designed as an additional safety and operational convenience feature for larger platforms such as the DEW-31, DEW-45, or DEW-63 Wafer Plants. It enables operators to control key machine functions from a safe distance, particularly useful in high-output environments where mobility and visibility are critical.
