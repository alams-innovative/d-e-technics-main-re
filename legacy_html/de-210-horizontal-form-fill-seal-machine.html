Product Description

Overview

The DE-210 is ideal for wrapping large-sized products such as buns, rusks-in-trays, cookies-in-trays, washing soap, toilet soap, and other similar items. Built to handle a wide range of heat-sealable films, this machine ensures quality pillow-type packaging with consistent sealing. It features a photo-electric eye for print registration and comes with castor-mounted base for easy mobility, making it efficient and flexible for industrial use.

Features

  • Handles bulkier items with precision
  • Compatible with most heat-seal films
  • Smooth and accurate film registration
  • Mobile design for flexible production layouts
  • Fast throughput of up to 150 packs/min

Technical Specifications

Size of Pack (L x W x H) 100–300 x 40–80 x 25–75 mm
Output Up to 150 packs per minute
Electric Motors (1) 1 H.P.
(2) ½ H.P.
Heaters 2.2 kW
Standard Equipment Photo Electric Eye & Inverter
Dimensions of Machine 4,130 x 1,050 x 1,600 mm
Weight 650 kg
Reel (Max Outer Dia / Core Dia) 300 / 75 mm

Applications

  • Bakery products in trays (buns, rusks, cookies)
  • Washing and toilet soap bars
  • General large-size pillow wrapping
