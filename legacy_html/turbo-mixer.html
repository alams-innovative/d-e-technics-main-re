Product Description

Overview

The Turbo Mixer by D.E. Technics is built for rapid and uniform mixing of ingredients into wafer batter. It ensures the batter has the correct texture and consistency required for high-quality wafer production. Designed with hygiene and performance in mind, this stainless steel mixer is equipped with high-speed rotating blades for thorough mixing in short cycles.

Its compact design, robust build, and easy discharge system make it a reliable choice for continuous production environments.

Features

  • High-speed and uniform mixing
  • Stainless steel construction
  • Simple operation and cleaning
  • Compact and durable design
  • Integrated with D.E. Technics' wafer lines
