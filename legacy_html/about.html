Our Company

D.E. Technics (Pvt.) Ltd. was established under the name of Dynamic Engineering in 1984. From the very beginning, the company adopted the policy of producing high-quality pouch packing machines and providing exceptional packaging in Lahore.

Our reputation as a leading packaging company in Lahore is built on our commitment to excellence and prompt after-sales service, which has helped us establish a strong and growing client base.

By integrating industrial automation, we ensure that our machines operate with maximum efficiency, reliability, and precision. Whether it's food packaging in Lahore or packaging for soaps, we use high-quality materials and proven electrical/electronic components to guarantee uninterrupted and trouble-free operations
