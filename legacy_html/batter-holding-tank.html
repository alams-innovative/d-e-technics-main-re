Product Description

Overview

The Batter Holding Tank by D.E. Technics is designed to store and maintain a consistent supply of batter used in wafer sheet production. Positioned between the Turbo Mixer and the baking section, it ensures a controlled feed of batter to the baking plates, reducing downtime and improving operational efficiency.

The tank is made of food-grade stainless steel with an easy-clean design and optional level control integration.

Features

  • Robust stainless steel build
  • Compact footprint
  • Facilitates continuous batter feed
  • Easy to clean and maintain
  • Integrated with D.E. Technics' wafer line systems
