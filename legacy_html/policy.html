What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be turned off in our systems.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization.
  • Targeting Cookies: These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant ads on other sites.

How to Manage Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can usually find these settings in the "Options" or "Preferences" menu of your browser. You can also delete cookies that have already been set.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Return Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2023

This Return Policy outlines the terms and conditions for returning products purchased from D.E. Technics (Pvt) Limited. We strive to ensure your satisfaction with our products and services.

Return Eligibility

You may return products purchased from us under the following conditions:

  • The product is in its original condition and packaging
  • The return is initiated within 30 days of delivery for standard products
  • The product is not a custom-made or specially ordered item
  • The product has not been used or installed

Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service department to obtain a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
  2. Package the product securely in its original packaging
  3. Include a copy of your invoice and the RMA number
  4. Ship the product to the address provided by our customer service department

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect the returned product, we will process your refund as follows:

  • For products in original condition: Full refund of the purchase price
  • For products with missing parts or packaging: Partial refund may be issued
  • For damaged products: No refund will be issued unless the damage occurred during shipping

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 10-15 business days after we receive and inspect the returned product.

Return Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, except in the following cases:

  • The product was delivered damaged
  • We shipped the wrong product
  • The product is defective (as confirmed by our technical team)

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Custom-made or specially ordered products
  • Products that have been used, installed, or modified
  • Consumable items (such as belts, filters, and lubricants)
  • Software and digital products

Exchanges

If you need to exchange a product for a different model or size, please follow the return process and place a new order for the desired product. We will process your refund for the returned product as described above.
